Is there a high correlation between stock performance and periodical PsychSignal Sentiment?

Business Logics

➡️ PsychSignal Sentiments: "Overbought " and "Oversold" signals.

  • Closing Price < Moving Average Lower Bound AND Bull-Bear < 10thpercentile-> “OB, Greed”.
  • Closing Price > Moving Average Upper Bound AND Bull-Bear > 90thpercentile-> “OS, Fear”.

➡️ Stock Performance 1: when “OverBought, Greed” signal appear. (If the stocks return is positive, we consider it as correct case)

  • 1-day: The stocks return for next 1 day is positive after "OB, Greed".
  • 2-day: The stocks return for next 2 day is positive after "OB, Greed".
  • 3-day: The stocks return for next 3 day is positive after "OB, Greed".
  • 5-day: The stocks return for next 5 day is positive after "OB, Greed".

➡️ Stock Performance 2: when “Overold, Fear” signal appear. (If the stocks return is negative, we consider it as correct case)

  • 1-day: The stocks return for next 1 day is negative after "OS, Fear".
  • 2-day: The stocks return for next 2 day is negative after "OS, Fear".
  • 3-day: The stocks return for next 3 day is negative after "OS, Fear".
  • 5-day: The stocks return for next 5 day is negative after "OS, Fear".
